Hotel Grand, situated in Torre del Pareis, offers a range of well-appointed rooms, many of which come with a balcony, satellite TV, and complimentary WiFi. They are equipped with essential amenities including a secure safe, a stocked minibar, and a private bathroom stocked with toiletries.

For those looking to stay active, Hotel Grand features a fitness center on-site. Additionally, Hotel Grand provides convenient services such as bicycle and car rental arrangements for its guests. The hotel's staff is also available to assist in planning excursions in the beautiful city of Palma de Mallorca.
